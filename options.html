Keyboard Shortcut

Current Shortcut: Command+K (Mac) / Ctrl+K (Windows/Linux)

To change the keyboard shortcut:

  1. Go to chrome://extensions/shortcuts
  2. Find "Quick URL Navigator"
  3. Click on the pencil icon next to "Open URL navigation modal"
  4. Press your desired key combination
  5. Click "OK" to save

How to Use

  • Press Command+K (or your custom shortcut) to open the modal
  • Type any URL (e.g., "google.com" or "https://example.com")
  • Press Enter to open the URL in a new tab
  • Press Esc to close the modal
